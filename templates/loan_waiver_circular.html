Loan Waiver Circular

Subject: Education Loan Waiver Scheme

Issued By: Higher Education Financial Authority

Objective: To provide financial relief to students from economically weaker sections by waiving off outstanding education loan amounts.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Loan sanctioned before January 1, 2023.
  • Borrower’s family annual income should be below ₹8,00,000.
  • Loan used exclusively for higher education purposes.

Benefits:

  • Complete or partial loan waiver depending on eligibility.
  • Reduced debt burden on students and families.
  • Opportunity to rebuild credit score post-waiver.

Application Process:

  • Submit waiver request to your lending bank along with supporting income and loan documents.
  • The bank will verify the eligibility and forward the application to the State Waiver Committee.
  • Sanction letter for waiver will be issued upon approval.

Note: Students meeting these criteria must apply through their lending bank before the deadline specified by the authority.
